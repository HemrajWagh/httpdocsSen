About The Megapolis Township

Megapolis offers Budget and Premium 1/2/3 BHK flats in hinjewadi . Likewise, home buyers can choose from Under Construction or Ready Possession Flats in Hinjewadi IT Park. The Township was conceived so that the rising number of computer software professionals working in the IT Park would not have to commute for long distances. Megapolis was established in 2008 as an integrated township for IT professionals. It is a joint venture together with Kumar Properties and ABIL. Consequently, it aims to construct contemporary townships.
